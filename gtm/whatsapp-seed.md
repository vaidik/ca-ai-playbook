## Targeting Notes

**Best groups to target:**
- ICAI member WhatsApp groups (city-level or national)
- Young CAs / CA students communities (they're already using AI — they'll buy)
- CA firm partners groups
- Tax practitioners' groups (GST-specific)
- CA CPE event WhatsApp groups

**Avoid:**
- Don't post in groups where you don't have credibility / front person doesn't have standing
- Don't post twice in the same group within a week (spam risk)
- Don't reply to your own post immediately to bump it — let it breathe

**Timing:**
- Best: Sunday evenings (when CAs check their phones)
- Second best: Monday morning
- Avoid: ITR/GST filing deadline days (everyone is swamped)
