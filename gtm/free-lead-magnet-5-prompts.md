## Prompt 1: GST – Summarise a Notification in Plain Hindi/English

**What it does:** Turns dense GST notifications into a 3-bullet summary you can share with clients instantly.

**The prompt (copy-paste ready):**
```
You are a GST expert. Summarise the following GST notification in 3 bullet points. Use simple English that a small business owner can understand. Include: (1) what changed, (2) who is affected, (3) what action they need to take. Then translate the same summary into Hindi.

Notification text:
[PASTE NOTIFICATION HERE]
```

**Expected output:** A clean, bilingual summary you can forward to clients on WhatsApp in under a minute.

## Prompt 2: Income Tax – Explain a Section to a Client

**What it does:** Translates legalese into a client-friendly explanation. Great for responding to "Is this deduction allowed?" type questions.

**The prompt (copy-paste ready):**
```
Act as a tax advisor explaining things to a non-finance client. In 4 to 5 sentences, explain Section [NUMBER] of the Indian Income Tax Act in simple terms. Include: what it covers, who can claim it, and any common mistake people make. End with one practical example.

Section: [e.g., 80C, 80D, 80G, 54, 54F]
```

**Expected output:** A client-ready paragraph you can paste directly into an email or WhatsApp reply. No more staring at a blank reply box.

## Prompt 3: Audit – Generate a Checklist from the Engagement Letter

**What it does:** Reads an audit engagement letter and extracts every deliverable, deadline, and document request into a checklist you can track.

**The prompt (copy-paste ready):**
```
Read the following audit engagement letter and create a checklist with 3 columns: Task, Owner, and Deadline. Include every explicit deliverable mentioned. Then add a second section listing all documents and records the auditor is requesting from the client.

Engagement letter:
[PASTE TEXT HERE]
```

**Expected output:** A structured checklist that ensures nothing falls through the cracks before field work begins.

## Prompt 4: Client Communication – Draft a Professional Late-Payment Reminder

**What it does:** Generates a firm-but-polite payment reminder that maintains the relationship while getting the invoice paid.

**The prompt (copy-paste ready):**
```
Write a polite but firm payment reminder email from a CA firm to a client. The invoice is [X] days overdue. Use a professional tone. Mention the invoice number and amount as placeholders. End with an offer to discuss if there are any issues. Keep it under 6 sentences.
```

**Expected output:** A ready-to-send email that sounds professional, not aggressive. Customise the placeholders and hit send.

---

## Prompt 5: Excel/Tally – Write a Formula or Troubleshoot an Error

**What it does:** You describe what you're trying to do in Excel or Tally, and AI writes the formula or finds the menu path. No more YouTube searches at 11pm.

**The prompt (copy-paste ready):**
```
I am using Microsoft Excel / Tally Prime. I want to [DESCRIBE WHAT YOU'RE TRYING TO DO, e.g., "flag all invoices older than 90 days in a column and highlight them red"]. Give me the exact formula or step-by-step menu path to do this. If there are multiple ways, show me the simplest one.
```

**Expected output:** The exact formula or click-by-click instructions. Works for conditional formatting, VLOOKUP mismatches, TDS reconciliation, aging reports, and more.
