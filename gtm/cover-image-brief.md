# Cover Image Brief

## For: Gumroad product thumbnail + WhatsApp preview

---

## Dimensions
- Gumroad: 1280 x 720px (landscape)
- WhatsApp preview: square crop safe (centre 720x720 should look good standalone)

## Visual Direction

**Style:** Clean, professional, dark background
**Vibe:** Like a premium Notion template or Gumroad digital product — not a textbook, not a government form

## Elements to Include

1. **Title text (large, prominent):**
   CA Prompt Pack

2. **Subtitle (smaller):**
   75 AI Prompts for Indian CAs

3. **Visual accent:** 
   A subtle grid of prompt cards or chat-bubble-style snippets floating in the background (ghosted/watermarked)
   OR: A simple document icon + the Claude/ChatGPT-style chat interface aesthetic

4. **Badge/callout (small):**
   "Works with free ChatGPT & Claude"

5. **Colour palette:**
   - Background: Deep navy (#0F172A) or charcoal (#1C1C1E)
   - Accent: Amber/gold (#F59E0B) or teal (#14B8A6)
   - Text: White / light grey

6. **Optional bottom bar:**
   "75 prompts · Client notices · Audit language · GST · Business development"
